ABOUT

We have created this space to connect the dots of artists, activists, scholars, organizations and archives that are engaged in archive-focus research-creation activities. More than providing links to people and places, we are working to map our connections, collaborations and relationships -- and create a digital space to support activities, share resources and encourage connections.



CREDITS

Hemispheric Encounters Developing Transborder Research-Creation Practices brings together, scholars, artists, activists, and community organizations from across the Americas to explore hemispheric performance as an artistic practice for addressing social and environmental justice.

The Performing Archives Stream of Hemispheric Encounters explores archive-focused research-creation activities that unlock and enliven the political potential of archives, documentation, and live performance. With special thanks to Laura Levin and Tracy Tidgwell.
